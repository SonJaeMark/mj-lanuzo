Frequently Asked Questions

What are your payment terms? +

I require 50% upfront to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion. For ongoing services, annual hosting and maintenance fees are billed in advance.

Do I own my domain and website? +

Absolutely! You own your domain name and all website content. I provide hosting and management services, but you maintain full ownership of your digital assets.

How long does it take to build a website? +

Most websites are completed within 2-4 weeks, depending on complexity and content readiness. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Can I update my website myself? +

Yes! I build websites with user-friendly content management systems. I also provide training and documentation, plus ongoing support for any updates you need help with.

What's included in your maintenance service? +

Maintenance includes security updates, performance monitoring, daily backups, uptime monitoring, minor content updates, and technical support via email and phone.
